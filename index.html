About me!

Enmanuel S.Minaya (Born January 30th,1991) A Dominican Rapper born and raised in New York City; a Songwriter and singer better known by his stage name Fa Melz. At a early age falls in love with music by joining a small Band named F.E.B MUSIC taking it as a hobby and recording his first song (Mi Cielo) at the age of 16. Later on becoming a solo artist and releasing his first Mix Tape(Blessings the Mixtape) in 2017; also working on his individual Record label HUSTLE MIND MUSIC.
